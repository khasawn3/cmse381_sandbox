\begin{frame}[t]{Single linkage}
\centering

Distance between cluster $A$ and cluster $B$:\\
Smallest distance between the points
\begin{equation*}
	L(A,B)= \min_{a \in A, b\in B}\|a-b\|
\end{equation*}
